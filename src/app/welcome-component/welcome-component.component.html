Welcome to Scrumptious!

Scrumptious was created to aid home cooking and minimize time inside grocery stores during COVID-19 pandemic.

All the scrumptious recipes listed are proven and we make regularly. The grocery list builder is designed to sort the various ingredients by the region found in the store. This should help minimize back and forth movement in the store, thus limiting time spent and exposure to other shoppers.

Thanks for taking a look and we hope you enjoy some of our favorite recipes!
